The Vision

SOHO systems mirror the way life organizes: nested wholes within wholes, each sovereign and yet in relationship. Applied to AGI, this becomes a planetary commons of intelligence — resilient, transparent, and governed by those it serves. No single center to capture. No brittle monolith to fail.

Principles: Distributed, resilient, community‑governed, aligned with living systems.
